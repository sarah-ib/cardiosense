Heart disease Analysis and Prediction using Machine Learning Techniques

Heart disease is among the most common causes of death worldwide, nowadays. However, early detection of cardiac problems and timely care of health practitioners can reduce the mortality rate. Therefore , a reliable system for diagnosing such pathologies is of utmost importance to be able to process an adequate treatment. The diagnosis of cardiac diseases is a challenging task and a crucial responsibility in the medical field, and it involves the utmost care. However, there exist techniques for extracting and analyzing data, and the availability of a large collection of medical data enables accurate diagnosis. By using medical records such age, sex, blood sugar, heart rate achieved, chest pain type and blood pressure, it is feasible to increase the likelihood of heart disease prediction.
